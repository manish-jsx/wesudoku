But you don't have to believe us! Every Sudoku puzzle we provide comes with its step-by-step solution, such as the hard example shown below. The step-by-step shows one way to solve puzzle using only logical reasoning. Naturally each puzzle can be solved in many different ways, but every way leads to the same solution.

With our step-by-step solutions, you can be confident that your readers will enjoy solving your Sudokus with pencil and paper, and will never be frustrated by logical dead ends.
