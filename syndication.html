Frequently Asked Questions


Do your Sudoku puzzles have one solution?

Yes, every Sudoku we create has a single solution, which can be reached by logic alone. But you don't need to take our word for it - we provide step-by-step guides for every puzzle we supply, so you can check the logic for yourself.


How do you grade the level of the puzzles?

Every puzzle is graded based on the depth of logical reasoning required. Our Sudokus never require 'brute force' or 'trial and error' methods, which are easy for computers but impossible for humans working with pen and paper.


How will I receive my Sudoku puzzles?

Our standard method of delivery is by email, within 48 hours of your order. We create a .zip file and send it to the address you specify. If you prefer we can also upload puzzles by FTP, or place the files online at a secret location for you to download.


Do you supply the same puzzles to multiple customers?

Absolutely not - we generate a new and unique set of puzzles for every order.


Have your Sudoku puzzles been tested?

Yes, millions of visitors come to WebSudoku.com every month to play our puzzles. Our Sudoku puzzles have also been syndicated in over 200 different publications in the USA, Europe and worldwide.
